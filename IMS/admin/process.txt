A basic process for a web-based inventory management system follows these steps:

User Authentication – Login system for admins, managers, and staff.
Dashboard Overview – Displays stock levels, recent transactions, and alerts.
Product Management – Add, update, delete, and categorize inventory items.
Stock In (Receiving Inventory) – Record new stock arrivals with quantity and supplier details.
Stock Out (Sales/Usage) – Deduct sold or used items from inventory.
Reorder Alerts – Notify when stock reaches a minimum threshold.
Reporting & Analytics – Generate stock movement reports, trends, and summaries.
User Roles & Permissions – Restrict access based on user roles.
Audit Logs – Track changes and transactions for accountability.
Backup & Security – Regular data backups and secure access controls.
